Pick-up and Delivery

All cakes handed to our clients are properly packaged. Our cakes keep perfectly fresh for five days.

Most orders can be picked-up between 7am and 5pm Monday to Friday, we do not deliver on Saturday, Sundays and Bank Holidays (wedding cake are exceptional). If your cake is needed at a specific time, it is best to pick-up a day before your due date.

Policy

Also please take a picture or keep a sample of the cake as this will help to resolve any problem or give a refund. A partial refund is determined by the nature of the problem, as discussed with the client at the time. Cancellations are possible one week before your occasion and two months for wedding cakes and specific cake orders. If you don't meet the deadline, a refund will be given based on the company's discretion.

Wedding cakes will be refunded but some subtraction may be made based on the company's expense incurred, due to what the company may have purchased or how much work may have gone into the creation of your individual cake.

All the information you provide is used solely by the company and is not transferred or used outside the company. We may contact you for marketing purposes by e-mail or telephone. If you do not wish to be contacted please indicate when you are placing an order.
